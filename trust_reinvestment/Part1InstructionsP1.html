Part 1 has {{ C.STAGE1_ROUNDS }} rounds. The same rules apply in every Part 1 round.

In each round, you receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} and choose how many points, from 0 to {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}, to send to player 2.

Player 2 receives three times the amount you send. After receiving this amount, player 2 chooses how many points to return to you.

In each round, earnings are calculated as follows:

your earnings = points kept from the endowment + points returned by player 2

player 2 earnings = amount player 2 receives - points returned to you

At the end of each round, your earnings from that round are added to your Part 1 accumulated account.

In each round, each player will see the other player's decision and both players' earnings for that round.
