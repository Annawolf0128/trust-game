Part 1 has {{ C.STAGE1_ROUNDS }} rounds. The same rules apply in every Part 1 round.

In each round, player 1 receives {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} and chooses how many points, from 0 to {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}, to send to you.

You receive three times the amount player 1 sends. After receiving this amount, you choose how many points to return to player 1.

In each round, earnings are calculated as follows:

player 1 earnings = points kept from the endowment + points returned by you

your earnings = amount you receive - points returned to player 1

At the end of each round, your earnings from that round are added to your Part 1 accumulated account.

In each round, each player will see the other player's decision and both players' earnings for that round.
