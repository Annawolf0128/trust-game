Your pair remains the same. The same rules apply in every Part 2 round.

In each round, you receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} and choose how many points, from 0 to {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}, to send to player 2.

Player 2 receives three times the amount you send.

You and player 2 will see the amount player 2 receives. Player 2 then chooses how many points to return to you.

your earnings = points kept from the current-round endowment + points returned by player 2

player 2 earnings = amount player 2 receives - points returned to you

At the end of each round, your earnings from that round are added to your Part 2 accumulated account.

Part 2 lasts for at least {{ min_rounds }} rounds. After round {{ min_rounds }}, at the end of each round there is a {{ stopping_probability }}% chance that Part 2 ends and a {{ continue_probability }}% chance that your pair continues for one more round.
