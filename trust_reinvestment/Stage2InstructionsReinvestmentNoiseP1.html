{{ block title }}Part 2 Rules{{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

Your pair remains the same. The same rules apply in every Part 2 round.

In each round, you receive a {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} endowment. You may send points to player 2 from this endowment and from your Part 2 accumulated account.

amount you send = points from the current-round endowment + points from your Part 2 accumulated account

The amount you send is multiplied by {{ C.MULTIPLIER }}, and player 2 receives the full multiplied amount. Player 2 then chooses how many points to return to you. The computer applies a random adjustment to the amount player 2 returns before it reaches you:

  • 80% chance: amount that reaches you = amount player 2 returns
  • 10% chance: amount that reaches you = amount player 2 returns x 0.5
  • 10% chance: amount that reaches you = amount player 2 returns x 1.5

your earnings = points kept from the current-round endowment + amount that reaches you from player 2

player 2 earnings = amount player 2 receives - points player 2 returns

At the end of each round, your earnings from that round are added to your Part 2 accumulated account, and any points you sent from that account are subtracted from it.

You see only the final amount that reaches you after the computer adjustment, not the amount player 2 chose to return.

Part 2 lasts for at least {{ min_rounds }} rounds. After round {{ min_rounds }}, at the end of each round there is a {{ stopping_probability }}% chance that Part 2 ends and a {{ continue_probability }}% chance that your pair continues for one more round.
