{{ block title }}Part 2 Rules{{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

Your pair remains the same. The same rules apply in every Part 2 round.

In each round, player 1 chooses how many points to send to you. Player 1 may send points from a current-round endowment of {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} and from player 1's Part 2 accumulated account.

The total amount player 1 sends is multiplied by {{ C.MULTIPLIER }}, and you receive the full multiplied amount. You then choose how many points to return to player 1. The computer applies a random adjustment to the amount you return before it reaches player 1:

  • 80% chance: amount that reaches player 1 = amount you return
  • 10% chance: amount that reaches player 1 = amount you return x 0.5
  • 10% chance: amount that reaches player 1 = amount you return x 1.5

player 1 earnings = points player 1 keeps from the endowment + amount that reaches player 1

your earnings = amount you receive - points you return to player 1

At the end of each round, your earnings from that round are added to your Part 2 accumulated account.

Player 1 sees only the final amount that reaches them after the computer adjustment, not the amount you chose to return.

Part 2 lasts for at least {{ min_rounds }} rounds. After round {{ min_rounds }}, at the end of each round there is a {{ stopping_probability }}% chance that Part 2 ends and a {{ continue_probability }}% chance that your pair continues for one more round.
